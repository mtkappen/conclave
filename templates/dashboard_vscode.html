Quick Guide 📖

  • Dungeon Master (DM): Creates campaign, runs the game, views all character sheets, manages members
  • Player: Creates characters and plays in campaigns
  • Spectator: Watches and chats without playing
  • Note: System Admin (superuser) can manage users via the Activity Bar but doesn't participate in campaigns by default.

Tips for Getting Started 💡

  1. Create a new campaign as a DM or join an existing one
  2. As a player, create your character sheet
  3. Use the chat feature to communicate with your party
  4. Roll dice directly in the campaign interface
  5. Check the sidebar for quick access to your campaigns and characters
